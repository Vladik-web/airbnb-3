Getting started with Calendar Sync

Even when you have published your property via Your.Rentals, you might still have one or more existing calendars that you are actively managing. By automatically synchronising all your calendars you’ll prevent multiple guests booking the same dates, and it saves you time as everything can be managed from one place.

In just a few steps we will help you set up a calendar sync for your listing, after which your calendar will be updated automatically.

How do you currently manage your calendar for this property?

You’re all set to start adding dates to your calendar!

By using the Your.Rentals calendar as the main calendar for your property, you only need to keep this calendar up-to-date. We’ll ensure the availability is correct in all channels via Your.Rentals.

Click next to start adding dates to your property’s calendar.

In the Availability tab, scroll down to Sync calendars and choose "export calendar"
