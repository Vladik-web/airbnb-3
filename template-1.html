Explore Dubai in a unique way

Palm Jumeirah is the largest man-made island on the coast of the Persian Gulf in Dubai. The island looks like a palm tree stretched out into the sea and consists of a trunk, 16 leaves and an 11-kilometer crescent. The resort is dotted with luxurious villas with private bays, gardens and swimming pools.

Palm Jumeirah is famous for its world-class attractions and abounds in luxurious restaurants and entertainment: Dolphin Bay, Aquaventure Water Park, aquariums, aquatic centers and fountains, as well as the legendary Atlantis hotels, observation decks and promenades.
