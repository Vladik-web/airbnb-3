How to get to the apartments

Here you can find a brief instruction on how to get to the apartments from the owner of the property

You can get there by public transport as follows. By commuter train RER B to Saint-Michel Notre-Dame station, there change to RER C to Champ de Mars - Tour Eiffel. You need to buy 1 ticket "to Paris" and use it at the entrance, at the transfer and at the exit. You don't need to buy additional tickets. The cost of a one-way ticket is 10.3 euros. Travel time - 1 hour.

There are also Air France buses, line 2, which travels directly to the Eiffel Tower. Cost: $18 one way, $31 round trip. Travel time - 1 hour.

There is a fixed price for a taxi to Paris. The trip to the Eiffel Tower will cost $55.
